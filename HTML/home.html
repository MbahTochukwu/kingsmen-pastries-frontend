About Us

Kingsmen Pastries is a Nigerian-owned pastry shop based in Onitsha-Ado, Anambra State. We take pride in offering a variety of freshly made Nigerian snacks and pastries crafted with love and tradition.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of pastries do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of Nigerian pastries including Puff Puff, Buns, Egg Rolls, Cake, Shawarma, Pizza, Small Chops, Samosa, and Sausage Rolls.

How do I place an order?

Click the "Order Now" button or visit our Order page to select your favorite pastries and provide your delivery details.

Do you deliver outside Onitsha?

Currently, we deliver within Onitsha and nearby areas. For special requests, please contact us directly.

How can I track my order?

Use the "Track Your Order" section on our homepage. Enter your order number to see the status of your delivery.

Can I customize my order?

Yes! For custom orders or special requests, please reach out via our Contact page or call us directly.
